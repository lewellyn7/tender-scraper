### 🔴 P0-2：N+1 优化方案落地

**依据：** `notes/API优化报告.md` — 优化项 #1

**问题：** `/api/projects` 分页 20 条 → 40 次 SQLite 查询（每个项目 is_favorite + annotation 各 1 次）

**优化方案：**
```python
# 改为 2 次批量查询替代 N×2 次单条查询
urls = [p.get("url", "") for p in page_projects]
placeholders = ",".join(["?"] * len(urls))
fav_map = {row["project_url"]: row for row in c.execute(
    f"SELECT project_url, status FROM favorites WHERE project_url IN ({placeholders})", urls
).fetchall()}
ann_map = {row["project_url"]: row for row in c.execute(
    f"SELECT project_url, note, priority FROM annotations WHERE project_url IN ({placeholders})", urls
).fetchall()}
```

**涉及文件：**
- `app/api/routes/projects.py`
- `app/database/db.py`

**验收标准：** 分页页面数据库查询 ≤5 次，API 响应时间 p99 -80%，pytest 回归通过
